Forex Done Right

At Institution X, founded in 2019 by Kevin Ndou and Innocent Mathebula, our mission is to empower individuals across South Africa with the knowledge and skills to excel in the forex market. With a commitment to education and mentorship, we have successfully guided over 500 individuals on their journey to becoming confident and profitable forex traders. Our institution stands as a beacon of financial literacy, fostering a culture of disciplined trading and risk management. We believe in democratizing access to forex trading, ensuring that anyone with the passion to learn can participate in this global marketplace. As a proudly South African institution, we are dedicated to promoting economic empowerment and financial independence. Our vision is to continue expanding our reach, nurturing a community of traders who not only navigate the forex market with confidence but also contribute positively to the growth and prosperity of our nation.
